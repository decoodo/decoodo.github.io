Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

Decodo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Wall Pix mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

Wall Pix is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers beyond what is required for advertising
  • We do not collect browsing history or app usage data
  • We do not require user accounts or login credentials

Advertising

Wall Pix is supported by advertisements. We use third-party advertising partners to display ads within the App. These advertising partners may collect certain information to provide personalized advertisements. This data collection is governed by the privacy policies of our advertising partners, not by Wall Pix directly.

Third-Party Advertising Services

Our advertising partners may use technologies such as cookies and advertising identifiers to deliver relevant ads. For more information about how these partners handle data, please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions

Wall Pix requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage Access: To save and set wallpapers on your device
  • Internet Access: To download wallpapers and display advertisements
  • Set Wallpaper: To apply wallpapers to your home screen and lock screen

These permissions are used solely for the functionality described and not for data collection purposes.

Local Data Storage

Wall Pix may store data locally on your device for the following purposes:

  • Saving your favorite wallpapers for quick access
  • Caching downloaded wallpapers to improve performance
  • Storing app preferences and settings

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Wall Pix does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is intended for general audiences and does not contain content specifically directed at children.

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy when using Wall Pix. Any data stored locally on your device is subject to your device's security measures.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete. If you wish to remove all app data, you can uninstall the application or clear the app's data through your device settings.
