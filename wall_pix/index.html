Frequently Asked Questions

Is Wall Pix free to use?

Yes, Wall Pix is free to download and use. The app is ad-supported, which helps us maintain and grow our wallpaper collection.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required! You can browse, download, and set wallpapers immediately after installing the app.

How often are new wallpapers added?

We regularly update our collection with fresh, high-quality wallpapers. New content is added frequently to keep your options diverse and exciting.

Does the app collect my personal data?

No. Wall Pix does not collect or share any personal data. We respect your privacy. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

Can I use these wallpapers on multiple devices?

Yes! You can download and use the wallpapers on any Android device where you have Wall Pix installed.

How does the auto wallpaper changer work?

The auto changer lets you set your wallpaper to automatically rotate from your favorites or a selected category at intervals you choose (hourly, daily, etc.).

What resolution are the wallpapers?

All wallpapers in Wall Pix are high-definition (HD) quality, optimized to look stunning on modern smartphone displays.

How do I contact support?

If you need help or have questions, please scroll down to the Support section or visit our contact page. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
