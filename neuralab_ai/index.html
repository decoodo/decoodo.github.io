How it Works

Typically, AI sounds like AI. Writing DNA fixes this by "cloning" your unique stylistic patterns.

  1. Input Samples: Paste 3–5 samples of your own best writing into the DNA Lab.
  2. Linguistic Analysis: The AI performs a "Stylistic Forensics" scan, analyzing your vocabulary, syntax, tone, and rhythm.
  3. DNA Profile Creation: The system distills these patterns into a "DNA Signature."
  4. Universal Application: Apply your DNA to tools like the Ghost Writer or Steal It to ensure the output sounds like you.

Essentially, Writing DNA removes the "AI Smell" from your content, giving you the speed of AI with the voice of a human expert.
