Privacy Policy for NeuraLab AI

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NeuraLab AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the features of NeuraLab AI (Selling, Dating Coach, Object Scanner, etc.), we require specific permissions to access data on your device.

A. Camera and Images

  • What we access: We access your device’s camera and photo gallery.
  • Why: This is required to capture or upload images for analysis by our AI tools (The Lab, Sell It, Rate Me, Scanner, etc.).
  • Storage: Images are processed securely. While "History" and "Scans" are saved locally on your device using secure encryption, images are transmitted to our cloud server temporarily for the sole purpose of AI analysis.

B. Authentication Data

  • What we access: We use Google Sign-In to authenticate your identity.
  • Why: To ensure secure access to the App’s features and manage your session.
  • Data: We may receive your basic profile information (such as your name or email) from Google to verify your account.

C. Device and Usage Data

  • What we access: We may collect information about your mobile device (e.g., operating system version) and how you interact with the App.
  • Why: To improve app stability, fix crashes, and display personalized advertisements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  1. To Provide Services: To analyze your uploaded images and generate text results (e.g., eBay descriptions, dating ratings, OCR text).
  2. To Maintain History: We store your past scans and generated results locally on your device so you can access them later.
  3. To Serve Ads: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements and Reward Ads to unlock Pro features.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

A. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may use your device's advertising ID to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

  • Link to Google AdMob Privacy Policy

B. Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In for authentication.

  • Link to Google Privacy Policy

C. Cloud Processing

We transmit image data to our secure cloud endpoint for AI processing. This data is used strictly for generating the analysis you requested and is not stored permanently on our servers.

4. Data Retention and Security

  • Local Storage: Your scan history, including images and text prompts, is stored locally on your device using FlutterSecureStorage and SharedPreferences. You can clear this history at any time via the App settings.
  • Security: We use standard encryption protocols (HTTPS) when transmitting data to our servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

NeuraLab AI is restricted to users aged 18 and older. We strictly enforce an age verification gate upon the first launch of the App. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
