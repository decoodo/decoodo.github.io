Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Welcome to NeuralLab AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure environment for all our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

When you use the Google Sign-In feature, we may collect:

  • Email Address: Used to identify your account and personalize your experience.
  • Profile Information: Such as your name and profile photo (held locally in the app for UI purposes).

B. Usage Data

We use Google Firebase for basic app analytics and crash reporting to improve app stability. This data is anonymized and does not include personal content.

C. Permissions & Device Data

To provide our forensic and content creation features, the app requests:

  • Storage/Media Access: To pick images for analysis and save generated assets.
  • Ad ID: For personalized advertising via Google AdMob.

2. Google Workspace Integration (Limited Use)

NeuralLab AI requests specific Google API scopes to allow you to save your data directly to your own Google Drive, Google Sheets, or Google Docs.

Scopes Used:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file: Allows the app to create and manage specific files you authorize.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets: Used to append content to your selected Google Sheets (e.g., Stock Metadata).
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents: Used to save generated text content (e.g., Ghost Writer) to your Google Docs.

How We Handle This Data:

  • No External Servers: Your content is sent directly from the app to the Google API endpoint. We do not store your spreadsheet data or document content on our own servers.
  • Limited Access: The app only accesses the specific files intended for the "Workspace Hub" features.
  • Compliance: NeuralLab AI’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and Analytics.
  • Google AdMob: Advertising.
  • Gen AI Services: Content analysis is processed using industry-standard AI models.
