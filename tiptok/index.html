Data Access & Permissions

TipTok requests Google permissions only to provide the sync features you enable.

📄

Google Sheets (spreadsheets)

Used to read and write spreadsheet content in your TipTok sheet for syncing (transactions, budgets, savings goals, subscriptions/recurring items, upcoming items, categories, and debts).
Scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets

🛡️

Local-first & user-controlled

TipTok does not host a backend server for your finance data. You can disconnect Google at any time, and your spreadsheet remains in your Google account.
