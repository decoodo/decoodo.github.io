Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the TipTok mobile application (“Application”) handles information.

This privacy policy applies to the TipTok app (hereby referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices that was created by TipTok (hereby referred to as the "Service Provider") as an ad-supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Data (Google Sheets):
The Application allows you to sync your financial data with your personal Google Sheets. The Application accesses this data only to display it within the app and to update your spreadsheet with new transactions. This data remains in your Google Drive and is never sent to the Service Provider's servers. We have no access to your spreadsheet data outside of the Application running on your device.

AdMob (Advertising):
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use personal data (such as your Advertising ID, device information, and usage data) to provide personalized ads.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Google Sheets: Your financial data is stored in your own Google Account. Google's privacy policy applies to data stored in Google Drive.

Ad Partners: We work with third-party advertising partners (like Google AdMob) who may access limited data (like device identifiers) to show relevant ads.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes available as part of your mobile device.

Children

The Application is not used to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Since your financial data is synced directly to Google Sheets, it benefits from Google's security infrastructure.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of changes by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

Effective date

This privacy policy is effective as of 2026-02-10.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by the Service Provider.
